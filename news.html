Here is our plan for the transition from Telkit 5.1.4 to Telkit 6.0.0

1. Mod Stabilization
Whilst all mods are updated that will be in the new pack, we will not be transitioning until those mods get a few stability updates. Telkit Development Edition will continue to be updated in the meantime. The biggest thing right now is that until Davinci's Vessels updates, we can't add in LiteLoader and VoxelMap. The other big thing is that Thaumcraft is crash-prone on the server side.

2. The New Server
As this is a transition across Minecraft versions, and as the new version will not have certain mods that are in the old version, this upgrade necessitates a new server world. We want all the mods that edit world generation in at the start, so once Thaumcraft and Buildcraft stabilize, we will begin testing a new world.

3. Rebuilding
Once the new world is built, certain things will be transitioned from the old server, whilst other buildings will be rebuilt or replaced entirely. Nosiphus Studios is among these, the new multi-building corporate campus plan will be used for the rebuild.
